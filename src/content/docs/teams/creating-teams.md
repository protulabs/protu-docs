title: Creating teams
description: Set up teams to unlock Team Intelligence.
---

Teams in Protu represent groups of people who work together.

## Overview

When you create a team and add members with completed assessments, you unlock Team Intelligence. This shows how the team works as a unit.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Teams** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Team**
3. Enter a team name
4. Add team members by selecting from your employees
5. Save the team

## Requirements

Team Intelligence requires at least 3 completed profiles. With fewer members, there is not enough data to analyse how patterns combine.

## Team settings

You can update team settings at any time:
- Rename the team
- Add or remove members
- Archive teams you no longer need
