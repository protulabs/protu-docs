Scenario views let you explore how your team would change if you added or removed members.

## Overview

Use scenarios to test hypothetical changes before making them. See how adding a candidate would shift team dynamics, or what would happen if a team member left.

## Creating a scenario

1. Open Team Intelligence for your team
2. Click **Add to scenario** on a person or candidate
3. The visualisation updates to show the projected impact

## Scenario types

### Adding someone
See how a candidate or employee from another team would fit. The visualisation shows their projected position and how existing dynamics would shift.

### Removing someone
Understand the impact of someone leaving. See which gaps would open and how the remaining team would need to adapt.

## Using scenarios

Scenarios help you:
- Evaluate candidates for team fit
- Plan for succession
- Understand key person dependencies
- Make informed team composition decisions
