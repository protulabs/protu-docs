Team Dynamics shows how each team member influences the team and how they relate to each other.

## Overview

While Team Composition shows what patterns exist, Team Dynamics shows how people interact. It reveals who anchors the team, who bridges different groups, and how influence flows.

## Reading the visualisation

The visualisation positions each team member based on their influence style:
- **Position** reflects their role in team dynamics
- **Connections** show relationships between members
- **Clusters** reveal natural working groups

## Key patterns

### Anchors
Some people anchor the team, providing stability and grounding. They often sit near the centre of the visualisation.

### Bridges
Bridges connect different parts of the team. They help information flow between groups and can translate between different working styles.

### Clusters
Natural working groups form based on complementary styles. Understanding these clusters helps with project assignments and communication.
