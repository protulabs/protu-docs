Team Composition shows how your team's behavioural patterns combine across all 10 markers.

## Overview

The Team Composition view displays your team's collective profile. It shows where your team has strength, where there are gaps, and how individual members contribute to the overall pattern.

## Reading the visualisation

The visualisation shows:
- **Team average** across each marker
- **Individual positions** showing where each person sits
- **Coverage** indicating how well the team covers each dimension

## Interpreting patterns

### Strengths
Markers where multiple team members score highly indicate team strengths. These are areas where your team has natural capability.

### Gaps
Markers with low coverage may indicate gaps. Consider whether these gaps matter for your team's work, and whether to address them through hiring or development.

### Diversity
Teams benefit from diverse perspectives. Look for markers where team members have different scores, as this can lead to richer discussions and better decisions.
