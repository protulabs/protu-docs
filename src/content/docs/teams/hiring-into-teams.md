title: Hiring into teams
description: Use team fit insights when evaluating candidates.
---

When hiring for an existing team, Protu shows how candidates would fit with your current team members.

## Overview

Link a role to a team to see team fit insights for each candidate. This helps you understand how a candidate's behavioural patterns would combine with your existing team.

## Linking a role to a team

1. Open the role you are hiring for
2. Click **Link to Team**
3. Select the team this role will join
4. Save

## What you will see

Once linked, candidate profiles show:
- How their patterns compare to the team
- What they would add to the team's composition
- Potential dynamics with existing team members
