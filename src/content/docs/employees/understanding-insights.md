This guide is for employees who have completed their Protu assessment.

## Your assessment

The Protu assessment is a 15-20 minute conversational experience. There are no right or wrong answers. The assessment captures authentic behavioural patterns from how you respond.

## Your profile

Your profile shows:
- **Markers** showing your patterns across 10 behavioural dimensions
- **Strengths** where you stand out relative to peers
- **Working style insights** about how you approach work

## Using your insights

Your profile can help you:
- Understand your natural working style
- Identify areas for development
- Communicate your preferences to colleagues
- Prepare for team discussions
