Employee profiles show behavioural insights for your team members.

## Overview

Each employee profile displays their behavioural patterns across all 10 markers, along with specific insights about their working style.

## What you will see

- **Marker overview** showing scores across all 10 dimensions
- **Stands out in** highlighting their relative strengths
- **Engage for** recommendations for when to involve them
- **Team memberships** showing which teams they belong to

## Using profiles

Employee profiles help you:
- Understand individual working styles
- Prepare for 1:1 conversations
- Identify development opportunities
- Make team assignment decisions
