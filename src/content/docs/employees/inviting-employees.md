Invite your existing team members to complete their Protu assessment.

## Overview

When employees complete their assessment, you can add them to teams and unlock Team Intelligence.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Employees** in the sidebar
2. Click **Invite Employee**
3. Enter the employee's email address
4. They will receive an invitation to complete the assessment

## What happens next

The employee receives an email with a link to complete their assessment. The assessment takes 15-20 minutes.

Once completed, their profile becomes available and you can:
- View their behavioural profile
- Add them to teams
- Compare them to candidates
