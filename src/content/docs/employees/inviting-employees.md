Before you can build teams and unlock Team Intelligence, your people need to complete their assessments. Here's how to get them started.

---

## Adding Employees

There are several ways to add employees to Protu, depending on how many you're adding.

### Invite by email

1. Go to **Employees** in the navigation (or press `E`)
2. Click **Invite employee**
3. Enter the employee's email address
4. Click **Send invite**

The employee receives an email with a magic link to complete their assessment. No password needed.

### Bulk invite

To invite multiple employees at once:

1. Go to **Employees**
2. Click **Invite employee**
3. Paste multiple email addresses (one per line, or comma-separated)
4. Click **Send invites**

### CSV import

For larger teams:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Employees**
2. Click **Import CSV**
3. Upload a CSV with email addresses
4. Review and confirm

---

## What Employees Experience

When invited, employees:

1. **Receive an email** from Protu with a magic link
2. **Click the link:** no account creation needed
3. **Complete the assessment:** 15-20 minutes, conversational format
4. **See their profile:** immediate access to their own insights

The assessment is not a test. There are no right or wrong answers. It captures natural behavioural patterns.

---

## Tracking Progress

Monitor where each employee is in the assessment process from the employees list.

### Employee statuses

| Status | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Invited** | Email sent, hasn't started |
| **Onboarding** | Opened the link, hasn't begun the assessment |
| **In progress** | Started the assessment |
| **Completed** | Assessment finished, profile available |

### Sending reminders

If an employee hasn't completed:

1. Go to their profile or the employees list
2. Click **Resend invite** (or **Send reminder**)
3. A new magic link is sent

**Tip:** A short personal message alongside the invite usually helps people follow through.

---

## After Completion

Once an employee completes their assessment:

- **Their profile is available:** View their behavioural markers and insights
- **They can be added to teams:** Select them when building or editing teams
- **Team Intelligence updates:** If they're on a team with 3+ completed members, intelligence recalculates
- **They see their own profile:** Employees have full access to their own insights

---

## Managing Employees

When someone leaves your organisation, you can deactivate or permanently delete their account.

### Deactivating

When someone leaves your organisation:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Employees**
2. Find the employee
3. Click **Deactivate**

Deactivated employees:
- Can no longer access their profile
- Are removed from active team calculations
- Historical data is preserved for reference

### Deleting

To permanently remove an employee's data:

1. Deactivate them first
2. Click **Delete** on the deactivated profile
3. Confirm deletion

You have 30 days to recover the data before it's permanently deleted.

For full details, see [Data Retention](/security/data-retention/).
