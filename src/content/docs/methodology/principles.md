Protu is built on established behavioural science principles.

## Behavioural patterns

People have consistent patterns in how they approach work, make decisions, and interact with others. These patterns are:
- Stable over time
- Observable in behaviour
- Meaningful for understanding work styles

## No right or wrong

There are no good or bad behavioural profiles. Different patterns suit different contexts. What matters is understanding patterns and how they combine.

## Team dynamics

When people work together, their individual patterns interact to create team dynamics. Understanding these dynamics helps teams work more effectively.

## Continuous development

Behavioural patterns are tendencies, not fixed traits. People can develop in areas that matter for their work and growth.
