title: Scoring
description: How Protu calculates behavioural scores.
---

Protu uses a validated approach to calculate behavioural scores.

## The assessment

The assessment is a 15-20 minute conversational experience. There are no right or wrong answers. Scores are derived from patterns in how you respond, not from specific answers.

## Markers

Protu measures 10 behavioural markers. Each marker represents a dimension of working style, such as how you approach decisions, handle uncertainty, or work with others.

## Score ranges

Scores range from 0 to 100 on each marker:
- **Higher scores** indicate stronger expression of that pattern
- **Lower scores** indicate less expression of that pattern
- **Mid-range scores** indicate moderate or context-dependent expression

## Reliability

Assessment results are validated for consistency and reliability. The conversational format helps capture authentic responses rather than socially desirable answers.
