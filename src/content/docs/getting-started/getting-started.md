Welcome to Protu. This guide helps you get started with the platform.

## Overview

Protu measures behavioural patterns to help you understand how people work, not just what they know or what they have done.

These patterns predict:
- How someone approaches problems
- How they work with others
- What environments help them thrive
- How they will affect a team's dynamics

## What you can do with Protu

- **Hire with confidence** by understanding how candidates will work with your team
- **Build effective teams** by seeing how behavioural patterns combine
- **Understand your people** through detailed behavioural profiles
