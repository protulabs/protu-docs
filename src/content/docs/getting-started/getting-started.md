## What is Protu?

Protu helps you hire better and build stronger teams by understanding how people actually work.

### The Problem Protu Solves

Hiring is broken. CVs tell you what people have done, not how they'll work. Interviews are inconsistent. Gut feelings lead to expensive mistakes.

Teams struggle too. You know something's off, but you can't articulate why. You hire someone great on paper who doesn't fit. You lose someone valuable you could have kept.

### What Protu Does

Protu reveals the behavioural patterns that predict how people work — not just what they can do, but how they'll do it.

**For Hiring:**
1. Assesses candidates through a short conversational experience
2. Shows you insights about how each person works
3. Compares candidates so you can make confident decisions
4. Predicts team fit so you hire people who'll thrive

**For Teams:**
1. Maps your team's dynamics — who drives, who stabilises, who connects
2. Identifies gaps and risks before they become problems
3. Shows hiring impact — how a new hire will change the team
4. Tracks trajectory — is your team getting stronger or drifting?

## Teams + Hiring Quickstart

**Goal:** Create a team, assess your people, then hire with full team comparison.

### Step 1: Create your first team (2 min)

1. Go to **Teams** in the navigation
2. Click **Create team**
3. Name it after a real team (e.g., "Product", "Engineering", "Sales")
4. Click **Create**

You now have an empty team ready for members.

### Step 2: Add yourself (1 min)

1. On the team page, click **Add employees**
2. Add yourself (search by name or email)
3. If you haven't completed an assessment, you'll be prompted to do so

**Tip:** Complete your own assessment first — it takes 15-20 minutes and helps you understand what candidates experience.

### Step 3: Invite your team (2 min)

1. Click **Invite members**
2. Enter email addresses for your team (one per line)
3. Click **Send invites**

They'll receive an email with a link to complete their assessment.

### Step 4: Wait for completions

Team Intelligence activates when **3+ members** complete their assessments. You'll see:
- Completion progress on the team summary
- Notifications as people complete
- Intelligence insights appearing as data comes in

**While you wait:** Move to Step 5 to set up your first role.

### Step 5: Create a role linked to your team (2 min)

1. Go to **Roles** in the navigation
2. Click **Create role**
3. Enter the role name (e.g., "Product Designer")
4. **Important:** Select your team in the team selector
5. Click **Create**

Linking to a team means candidates will be compared against your actual team composition.

### Step 6: Invite candidates (1 min)

1. On the role page, click **Invite candidates**
2. Enter candidate email addresses
3. Click **Send invites**

Candidates receive an email with a link to complete their assessment.

### Step 7: Review and decide

Once candidates complete:
1. Click a candidate to see their profile
2. Review their behavioural insights
3. Check **team comparison** to see how they'd affect your team
4. When ready, click **Select** on your chosen candidate
5. Click **Confirm hire** to complete the process

**You've just made your first data-driven, team-aware hiring decision.**

## Hiring Only Quickstart

**Goal:** Create a role, assess candidates, and make a confident hiring decision.

### Step 1: Create your first role (2 min)

1. Go to **Roles** in the navigation
2. Click **Create role**
3. Enter the role name (e.g., "Senior Engineer")
4. Add an optional description
5. Click **Create**

Your role is now active and ready for candidates.

### Step 2: Invite candidates (1 min)

1. On the role page, click **Invite candidates**
2. Enter candidate email addresses (one per line, or comma-separated)
3. Click **Send invites**

Each candidate receives an email with a link to complete their assessment.

### Step 3: Wait for completions

Candidates typically complete within 24-48 hours. You'll see:
- Status updates in your pipeline (Invited → In progress → Completed)
- Notifications when candidates complete
- Insights appear immediately after completion

### Step 4: Review candidate insights (2 min per candidate)

Once a candidate completes:
1. Click their name to see their profile
2. Review their **behavioural markers** (Leadership, Accountability, etc.)
3. Read their **key insights** — what they're strong in, how they work
4. Check the **leading trait** for their dominant pattern

### Step 5: Compare candidates

With multiple completed candidates:
1. Review each profile
2. Note differences in their marker patterns
3. Consider what the role actually needs
4. Think about working style fit, not just "highest scores"

### Step 6: Make your decision

1. Click **Select** on your chosen candidate
2. Review your choice (you can change before confirming)
3. Click **Confirm hire** to finalise

**You've just made your first insight-driven hiring decision.**

## How It Works

### The Assessment

Candidates complete a 15-20 minute conversational assessment. No trick questions, no right answers — just natural responses that reveal working style. Learn more about [how scoring works](/methodology/scoring/).

### The Insights

Protu translates responses into **behavioural markers** — patterns like accountability, adaptability, creativity, and collaboration. These aren't personality labels. They're observable working behaviours. See [Key Concepts](/getting-started/key-concepts/) for the full list of markers.

### The Intelligence

Raw data becomes actionable insight:
- **For candidates:** How they'll approach work, what environments suit them, where they'll excel
- **For teams:** Composition, dynamics, gaps, and trajectory — see [Team Intelligence](/teams/intelligence/team-intelligence/)
- **For decisions:** Clear signals, not vague impressions

## What Makes Protu Different

### Intelligence, Not Admin

Protu doesn't just store data — it surfaces insights you wouldn't find yourself. The platform tells you what matters, not just what happened.

### Discovery, Not Declaration

You don't have to label teams or categorise people. Protu discovers patterns from behaviour, not from boxes you tick.

### Transparency

Every insight shows what it's based on. No black boxes. You can always see why Protu is telling you something.

### Team Context

Most hiring tools evaluate candidates in isolation. Protu shows how candidates affect your specific team — because fit depends on who they're joining.

## Common Early Questions

### How long does the assessment take?

15-20 minutes for candidates and employees. It's conversational, not a test.

### What if candidates don't complete?

Send a reminder from the role page. Most complete within a day of a reminder.

### Can I see my own results?

Yes. Complete an assessment yourself to see exactly what candidates experience.

### What makes a "good" candidate?

There's no universal good or bad. It depends on what your role and team need. Protu shows you how people work — you decide what fits.
