This guide walks you through creating your first role in Protu.

## Overview

A role represents a position you are hiring for. When candidates complete their assessment for a role, you can see how their behavioural patterns compare to your team.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Roles** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Role**
3. Enter a role title and description
4. Optionally link the role to a team for team fit insights
5. Save the role

## Inviting candidates

Once your role is created:
1. Open the role
2. Click **Invite Candidate**
3. Enter the candidate's email address
4. They will receive an invitation to complete the assessment

## What you will see

After candidates complete their assessment, you will see:
- Their behavioural profile across all 10 markers
- How they compare to your team (if linked)
- Specific insights about their working style
