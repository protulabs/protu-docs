---

## The Core Idea

Protu measures **behavioural patterns** — how people actually work, not what they know or what they've done.

These patterns predict:
- How someone approaches problems
- How they work with others
- What environments help them thrive
- How they'll affect a team's dynamics

---

## Markers

**Markers** are Protu's 10 high-level behavioural groupings. Think of them as the main dimensions of working style. For details on how markers are calculated, see [How Scoring Works](/methodology/scoring/).

| Marker | What it captures |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Growth Mindset** | Orientation toward learning, embracing challenges, and development |
| **Leadership** | Ability to guide, influence, and inspire others |
| **Creativity** | Novel ideas, innovative thinking, and exploring possibilities |
| **Open-mindedness** | Receptiveness to different perspectives and tolerance of ambiguity |
| **Accountability** | Ownership, follow-through, and personal standards |
| **Perseverance** | Persistence, focus, and resilience under pressure |
| **Social Awareness** | Reading social dynamics, empathy, and interpersonal skills |
| **Teamwork** | Collaboration, relationship building, and group contribution |
| **Transparency** | Open communication, honesty, and information sharing |
| **Self-awareness** | Understanding own emotions, strengths, and limitations |

Each person has a pattern across all 10 markers. There's no "good" or "bad" pattern — different roles and teams benefit from different combinations.

---

## Submarkers

**Submarkers** are the 80+ granular behavioural dimensions that sit beneath markers.

For example, under **Teamwork**, you might see:
- Collaboration
- Communication
- Inclusivity
- Conflict resolution
- Support

Submarkers give you detailed insight when you need it, while markers give you the big picture.

---

## Influence Levels

For each submarker, Protu assesses the person's **influence level** — the scope of potential impact they could have.

| Level | Name | What it means |
|-------|------|---------------|
| 4 | **Supercharge** | Could have organisation-wide transformational impact |
| 3 | **Optimise** | Could drive department or function-level improvement |
| 2 | **Enable** | Strong team-level contribution |
| 1 | **Support** | Solid individual/role-level foundation |

Higher isn't always better. A team of all "Supercharge" individuals might clash. Different levels play different roles.

---

## Impact Levels

**Impact levels** describe observed behavioural strength — where someone currently sits in each dimension.

| Level | Name | What it means |
|-------|------|---------------|
| 5 | **Exceptional** | Among the strongest in this dimension |
| 4 | **Standout** | Notably strong, often visible to others |
| 3 | **Progressing** | Solid capability, continuing to develop |
| 2 | **Developing** | Building capability, room to grow |
| 1 | **Starting** | Early stage, significant growth opportunity |

Impact levels show current state. Influence levels show potential scope of effect. Together they paint the full picture.

---

## Behavioural Energy

Protu groups behavioural patterns into two broad energies:

| Energy | What it looks like | Markers that contribute |
|--------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **Drive** | Achievement-oriented, pushes for progress, takes initiative | Leadership, Perseverance, Growth Mindset |
| **Stability** | Grounding, maintains calm, supports consistency | Self-awareness, Accountability, Social Awareness |

Most people have both energies — the balance varies. Teams benefit from a mix of both. A team heavy on Drive may move fast but burn out. A team heavy on Stability may be reliable but slow to change.

## Team Intelligence

When you add team members to Protu, you unlock **Team Intelligence** — insights about how the team works as a unit. See [Team Intelligence](/teams/intelligence/team-intelligence/) for the complete guide.

### Team Composition

The behavioural makeup of your team. Who's on it and what patterns they bring together.

### Team Dynamics

How the individual patterns interact. Is there balance? Tension? Gaps?

### Team Gaps

Behavioural areas where your team lacks coverage. Not necessarily problems — but worth knowing.

### Team Strengths

Areas where your team has strong coverage or standout individuals.

### Bridges

A **Bridge** (or Connector) is someone who belongs to two or more teams. Bridges are valuable because they:
- Carry information and context between teams
- Spot cross-team dependencies early
- Create informal coordination that org charts miss

Protu automatically identifies bridges and surfaces insights about their cross-team position. Learn more in [Team Intelligence](/teams/intelligence/team-intelligence/).

## Profile Insights

### Stands Out In

When Protu says someone **stands out** in a submarker, it means their score is notably higher than peers — typically top 15% or better. Standouts represent genuine relative strengths, not just high scores.

### Engage For

**Engage for** recommendations suggest situations where someone's behavioural strengths make them particularly valuable. For example: "Engage Sarah when decisions are stalling" — because her Decisiveness stands out.

## Discovery vs Declaration

Protu **discovers** patterns — it doesn't ask you to declare them.

| Traditional tools | Protu |
|-------------------|-------|
| "Select your team type" | Discovers team archetype from behaviour |
| "Assign a team lead" | Identifies natural influence patterns |
| "Tag this team as..." | Surfaces characteristics from data |

This means less admin for you, and patterns that reflect reality rather than intentions.

---

## Comparison, Not Fit

When evaluating candidates, Protu shows how they **compare** to your team — not whether they "fit."

Why the distinction?
- **Fit** implies conformity (hire people like us)
- **Comparison** shows impact (hire people who change us in specific ways)

Sometimes you want similar. Sometimes you need different. Protu shows you both so you can decide.

---

## Insights vs Scores

Protu emphasises **insights** over raw scores.

| Raw score | Insight |
|-----------|---------|
| "Accountability: 72" | "Takes ownership of outcomes and follows through consistently" |
| "Team fit: 0.85" | "Would add structured thinking the team currently lacks" |

Numbers are available when you need them. But the insight tells you what the number *means*.

---

## The Assessment

The Protu assessment is a 15-20 minute conversational experience.

**What it is:**
- Natural, open-ended responses
- No right or wrong answers
- Captures authentic behavioural patterns

**What it isn't:**
- A test you can pass or fail
- A measure of intelligence or competence
- Something you can game with "correct" answers

The assessment works because it captures *how* people respond, not just *what* they say.

---

## Transparency

Every insight in Protu shows what it's based on.

- Click any marker to see the submarkers beneath it
- Click any insight to see the data behind it
- See confidence levels when data is limited

No black boxes. You can always understand why Protu is telling you something.

## Quick Reference Glossary

Alphabetised lookup for all key terms:

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Bridge** | An employee who belongs to 2+ teams, connecting them |
| **Candidate** | Someone being assessed for a hiring role |
| **Confidence level** | How reliable Team Intelligence is (High/Medium/Low based on completion %) |
| **Connector** | Same as Bridge |
| **Drive energy** | Achievement-oriented behavioural patterns (Leadership, Perseverance, Growth Mindset) |
| **Employee** | A team member who has completed (or been invited to) an assessment |
| **Engage for** | Situations where someone's strengths are particularly valuable |
| **Impact level** | Behavioural magnitude of a marker (Exceptional → Starting) |
| **Influence level** | Scope of potential effect (Supercharge → Support) |
| **Marker** | One of 10 high-level behavioural dimensions |
| **Overlay** | A "what-if" comparison showing how someone would affect a team |
| **Role** | A hiring position you're recruiting for |
| **Stability energy** | Grounding behavioural patterns (Self-awareness, Accountability, Social Awareness) |
| **Stands out in** | Submarkers where someone scores notably higher than peers |
| **Submarker** | One of 80+ granular behaviours within markers |
| **Team Intelligence** | Aggregate insights about how a team works together |
