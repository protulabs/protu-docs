This guide walks you through creating your first team in Protu.

## Overview

Teams in Protu represent groups of people who work together. When team members complete their assessments, you unlock Team Intelligence, which shows how the team works as a unit.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Teams** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Team**
3. Enter a team name
4. Add team members by selecting from your employees
5. Save the team

## Requirements for Team Intelligence

Team Intelligence requires at least 3 completed profiles. With fewer members, there is not enough data to analyse how patterns combine.

## What you will see

Once your team has 3+ completed profiles, you will see:
- **Team Composition** showing your team's behavioural fingerprint
- **Team Dynamics** showing how each person shapes the team
- **Scenario views** to explore what-if situations
