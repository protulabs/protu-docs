title: Passwordless access
description: Secure, password-free authentication.
---

Protu uses passwordless authentication to keep your account secure.

## How it works

Instead of passwords, Protu sends a secure link to your email address each time you sign in. Click the link to access your account.

## Benefits

Passwordless authentication:
- Eliminates password-related security risks
- Removes the need to remember passwords
- Prevents credential stuffing attacks
- Simplifies the sign-in experience

## Signing in

1. Enter your email address
2. Check your inbox for the sign-in link
3. Click the link to access Protu

Links expire after a short period for security.
