title: Privacy
description: How Protu handles personal data.
---

Protu is designed with privacy at its core.

## Data collection

The Protu assessment collects behavioural responses through a conversational interface. We do not collect:
- Biometric data
- Health information
- Protected characteristics

## Data ownership

Your organisation owns its data. Protu processes data on your behalf to provide insights.

## Individual rights

Employees can:
- View their own profile
- Understand how their data is used
- Request data export

## Retention

Assessment data is retained for as long as your organisation maintains an active account. When an account is closed, data is deleted according to our retention policy.
